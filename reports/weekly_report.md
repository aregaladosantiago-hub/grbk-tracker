## How to read it

- **Active up + removals low** = inventory building / slower sell-through.
- **Active down + removals high** = cleaner demand signal.
- **High or rising price-cut ratio** = pricing pressure / possible margin risk.
- **High removals + high price-cut ratio** = homes are moving, but likely with price support.

## Notes

- This is a public-listing flow tracker, not official GRBK orders or closings.
- Removed listings are a sell-through proxy. A home can also disappear because of relisting, URL changes, or temporary website changes.
- The first snapshot only establishes the baseline; the signal improves after several daily snapshots.
